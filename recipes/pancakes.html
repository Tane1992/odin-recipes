here you will find ingredients on how to make pancake mix mmmm yum!

ingredients

  1. milk
  2. eggs
  3. flour
  4. sugar
  5. more eggs i think

steps to make pancakes

  1. crack eggs to mix in a bowl of your choosing (i recommend not choosing ceramic bowls the noise is infuriating).
  2. number two put all the ingredients into one bowl and mix thoroughly.
  3. tip all of your mix into a pan that you should of been pre heating and go and sit in your car. if you didnt pre heat your pan then post heat it i guess.
  4. while in your car proceed to drive to the super market preferably one around the corner.(wear your seatbelt)
  5. because you dont have a supermarket nearby proceed to drive to another suburb
  6. go in to the super market to buy maple syrup and wait at the checkout for counter service
  7. realise you left your wallet at home
  8. put maple syrup back and return to car
  9. drive home and return to the kitchen to find your pancake is black on all fours
  10. be sad because you have no maple syrup and cant be bothered driving back to the super market
  11. scrape all the burned charcoal like substance from your pancake
  12. throw pancake in the bin and eat charcoal
